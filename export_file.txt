Original Text
-------------
O fetita a fost surprinsa intr-o fotografie in timp ce isi incarca inhalatorul
intr-o benzinarie din Kiev. Imaginea a fost distribuita pe Twitter de Lesia

Vasylenko, deputat in parlamentul Ucrainei.

"Fetita aceasta a trebuit sa mearga la o benzinarie pentru a-si incarca
inhalatorul si a-1 mentine in functiune. Este o imagine la fel de
impresionanta ca cea cu operatia pe cord pe care medicii au facut-o unui
copil in timpul bombardamentelor. Viata continua, dar cei mai vulnerabili

sunt in cel mai mare pericol', a scris Lesia Vasylenko.



Translation
-----------
A little girl was caught in a photo while loading her inhaler
in a gas station in Kyiv. The image was shared on Twitter by Lesia

Vasylenko, deputy in the parliament of Ukraine.

"This girl had to go to a gas station to fill up
the inhaler and keep it working. It is an image as well
impressive as the one with the heart surgery that the doctors did to one
child during the bombings. Life goes on, but the most vulnerable

I am in the greatest danger', wrote Lesia Vasylenko.
